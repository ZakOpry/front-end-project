About Guitar Master:

GuitarMaster.com is a site for guitarists of all experience levels, designed by a guitarist.

Our simple yet elegant design was created with guitarists in mind to get you playing and learning right away.

Search through thousands of tabs for your favorite songs and artists. If you are new to tabs, we have created the "Learning" page to get you on the fast track to playing.

So tune up and get jammin!
